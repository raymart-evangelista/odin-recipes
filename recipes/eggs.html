Soft boiled eggs

Description

These boil eggs will be soft boiled and have a mostly runny yolk. The reason why we want a running yolk is to ensure that we are optimizing the protein and content gains of the eggs. We lose more nutrition value when the egg yolk is hard

Because we want to maximize gains, we want a runny yolk, which will be perfect for the muscle gains.

Ingredients

  • 3 uncooked eggs
  • pot
  • water

Steps

  1. Fill the pot with 3/4 water
  2. Put pot on stovetop and set stovetop heat to high
  3. Add uncooked eggs into pot
  4. Once the water starts to boil, start a timer for 6 minutes
  5. After 6 minutes, remove pot from stovetop and pour cold water into pot
  6. Continue pouring cold water into pot for 2 minutes
  7. Use a spoon and remove the shell form the egg
  8. Serve eggs
