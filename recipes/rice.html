Rice

Description

This recipe will guide you to making the perfect amount of rice. We don't want dry rice or too wet rice where the rice won't be nice to look at.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • rice cooker
  • water

Steps

  1. place 1/3 cup of rice into rice cooker pot
  2. add cold water and use hands to mash rice in order to remove starch
  3. pour cold water out while keeping rice in pot
  4. continue to add cold water, mash rice, and pour cold water out until the water is mostly clear when mashing the rice
  5. try to remove as much water from the pot
  6. place 1 cup of water into the pot
  7. place pot into rice cooker and set to cook
  8. after 30-45 minutes, the rice should be cooked to perfection
